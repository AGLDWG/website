Linked Data WG Presentations

Here are some presentations about Linked Data given by members of this group and other Linked Data experts

  • 2019 Sep 11 & 12 Event: Next Big Tech Asia Conference Kuala Lumpur, 2019
    • League of Data
      • Presenter: Nicholas Car
      • About: Following-up on 2018's NextBigTech presentation about the use of Linked Data by Australian government
  • 2018 Oct 2&3 Event: Next Big Tech Asia Conference Kuala Lumpur
    • How Big Data Can Elevate Better Public Service Delivery
      • Presenter: Nicholas Car
      • About: Big Data impacts on Public/Private collaboration, Analytics for public service delivery, Real-time data adoption
    • The Australian Case Study: Governance of Federated, Federal Data
      • Presenter: Nicholas Car
      • About: The Australian data landscape, Distributed data production, Interoperability, Data harmonisation & integrity, aims for industry
  • 2018 July 20 New methods of governing Linked Data
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: Gov Open Data Meet-up, ABS videoconference
    • About: the AGLDWG's current work
  • 2018 Jun 13 Event: ANZLIC Metadata Working Group, Canberra
    • Metadata and Linked Data: Where is it all going?
      • Presenter: Nicholas Car
      • About: how spatial catalogue managers in Australia can implement metadata elements within their current catalogue installation that allow the catalogues information to be interpreted as RDF/Linked Data
    • How can you contribute to the W3C and the AGLDWG?
      • Presenter: Armin Haller
      • About: the AGLDWG, LD in general, LD internationally and some spatial LD work like SSN ontology
  • 2018 May 30 Persistent URIs for Australian Government
    • Presenter: Simon Cox
    • Event: C3DIS Conference, Melbourne
    • About: the AGLDWG's previous persistent URI system and the plans for a new one
  • 2018 May 23 Whole-of-government data integration using Linked Data
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: Geoscience Information Consortium, Canberra
    • About: How the AGLDWG is helping to achieve national-scale, multi-agency data integration using Linked Data
  • 2018 March 27 Australian Government Linked Data Working Group (AGLDWG)
    • Presenter: Armin Haller
    • Event: Webinar series on Linked Data programmes of national administrations, Scottish Government
    • About: Who the AGLDWG is and what it does
  • 2017 Aug 15 Open Data - Link It!
    • Presenter: Armin Haller
    • Event: 2017 Australian Computer Society Canberra Branch Conference
    • About: fundamentals of Linked Data, schema.org, government LD use, the AGLDWG
  • 2017 Aug 03 Member agency's LD status
    • Presenter: Multiple AGLDWG members
    • Event: AGLDWG AGM 2017
    • About: update on AGLDWG member agency's LD activities in the last year
  • 2017 Jul 30 A current Linked (Open) Data System at Geoscience Australia
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: 2017 June Open Data Community Forum
    • About: a demo of a current LD system at Geoscience Australia
  • 2017 Jul 20 Libraries & Linked Data - From a Linked Data point of view
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: 2017 Aust. Parliamentary Libraries Assoc. Conference
    • About: thoughts on libraries' role in LD and LD in general
  • 2017 Apr 05 Australia's distributed national spatial dataset production systems and community
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: WWW2017 World Wide Web conference, W3C session
    • About: current state of LD adoption in the Australian spatial government sector
  • 2017 Mar 25 Examining best practice methods for publishing structured data and linking government data
    • Presenter: Armin Haller
    • Event: 2017 Australian Government Data Summit
    • About: Why should we use LD to publish public sector information?
  • 2017 Mar 22 The Australian Government Linked Data Working Group
    • Presenter: Nicholas Car
    • Event: 2017 March Open Data Community Forum
    • About: short intro to the AGLDWG
