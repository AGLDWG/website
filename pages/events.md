# Australian Government Linked Data Working Group

## AGLDWG Monthly Meetings

The WG meets online on the first Thursday of every month, 4-5pm Canberra time, other than in January.

To get notification for these meetings and teleconference details, [join the mailing list](/join).

## Speaker Schedule

The WG invites experts on Linked Data and related fields to present during Monthly Meetings and in other special sessions.

Our [Speaker Schedule for 2026 is online](https://github.com/AGLDWG/meeting-minutes/blob/master/plenaries-agenda-2026.md).

Highlights for 2026 are:

* a special session on [Verifiable Credentials](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Verifiable_credentials) in March
* a series of presentations on [SOLID](https://solidproject.org/), Feb - May
* a special session on [SHACL](https://w3c.github.io/data-shapes/shacl12-overview/)
    * by many of the W3C SHACL editors
* international government Linked Data presenters, Apr - Oct
